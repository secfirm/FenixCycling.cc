What We Do

FULL-SERVICE
BIKE REPAIR

From a flat fix to a complete overhaul — we handle it all. Every job gets the same attention, whether it's a kid's first bike or a race build.

Tune-Ups

Keep your bike running like new. We clean, adjust, and inspect everything that matters.

  • Basic tune-up — adjust derailleurs, brakes, lube chain
  • Complete tune-up — full drivetrain service + safety check
  • Pre-ride safety inspection
Wheels

Truing, builds, and tubeless conversions. We work on all wheel sizes and standards.

  • Wheel truing
  • Custom wheel builds
  • Tubeless conversion (front & rear)
  • Spoke replacement
Brakes

Hydraulic or mechanical — we bleed, adjust, and replace. Stopping power you can trust.

  • Hydraulic brake bleed
  • Brake pad replacement
  • Cable & housing replacement
  • Rotor truing & replacement
Drivetrain

Chain, cassette, chainrings, derailleurs. We diagnose shift issues and fix them right the first time.

  • Chain & cassette replacement
  • Derailleur adjustment & overhaul
  • Bottom bracket service
  • Crankset installation
Suspension

Fork and rear shock service by a mechanic who rides. Proper setup makes everything else better.

  • Fork lower leg service
  • Fork overhaul
  • Rear shock overhaul
  • Suspension setup & tuning
À La Carte

Just need one thing done? Flat fix, cable swap, bar tape, headset press — we handle individual jobs too.

  • Flat repair (tube or tubeless)
  • Handlebar tape & grips
  • Headset & stem service
  • New bike assembly

Our Story

BUILT ON
HONEST
SERVICE

FENIX Cycling has called Yorba Linda home since 2012 — on the edge of Anaheim Hills and Corona, where the roads meet the trails and serious riders live. Riders from Riverside to the OC coast make the drive, because when you want it done right, you find the right mechanic.

That mechanic is Ozzy Hernandez. He didn't just grow up around bikes — he raced them professionally in the United States for five years. Cycling isn't a job at FENIX. It's a family tradition, and that heritage shows in every repair that leaves this shop.

Ozzy brings a racer's eye to every build and service — the kind of attention to detail that only comes from knowing exactly what's at stake when a bike fails on the road or trail.

No upsells you don't need. No mystery charges. Just the honest workmanship that earned us a 5.0 on Google and 4.8 on Yelp across 240+ combined reviews.

We ride. We wrench. We care.
And we treat every bike like our own.
